About our Company

J.D. ENTERPRISES

J.D. ENTERPRISES is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company established in 2006 and engaged in the Design, Manufacture and Supply of a variety of transformers. We have 17 years of experience in the Design & Manufacturing of Transformers with a very clear objective of providing the best Quality, on-time delivery with Customer Satisfaction.
Our vast range of transformer manufacturing includes - Industrial Double Wound Control, Step Down, Step Up, Isolation, 3 Phase Transformer, Line Choke, Auto Transformers, Ultra Isolation, Multi–Tapping Transformer, Current Transformer and many more.
The mission of J.D. ENTERPRISES is to be a flexible partner for our clients in the electrical and power sector by offering the most dependable, highest-quality products, and affordable services. J.D. ENTERPRISES is dedicated to achieving 100% client satisfaction by constantly improving its processes this has made it possible for us to compete in terms of Quality, Cost, and Delivery.
